# Activate Mental Focus By Ensuring the below points

- Get everything ready to prevent interruption: Water/tea/coffee, pre-bathroom break, charger, pen, and paper.
- Remove distraction: Put phone somewhere else or switch it off
- Tell others that you are working and will show up later
- Plan what you will be working on
- Set a timer of 2-3 hours.
- Get in a learning/working head space
- Be mindful/meditate for a minute beforehead
- Play a music with no lyrics.
